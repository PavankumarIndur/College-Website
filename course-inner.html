Course Overview

This comprehensive course provides an in-depth understanding of both finance and marketing principles, equipping students with essential skills to make informed financial decisions and develop effective marketing strategies.


Key Topics:

Financial Analysis: Learn to analyze financial statements, ratios, and performance metrics to make sound investment decisions.


Investment Strategies: Explore various investment options, portfolio management techniques, and risk mitigation strategies.


Digital Marketing: Gain expertise in leveraging digital channels like social media, SEO, and content marketing to enhance brand visibility.


Consumer Behavior: Analyze consumer needs and buying behavior to develop targeted marketing campaigns.


Brand Management: Develop strategies for building and maintaining a strong brand presence.


What you'll learn

Financial decision-making and investment analysis.

Risk assessment and portfolio management.

Strategic marketing planning and execution.

Digital marketing and consumer insight
