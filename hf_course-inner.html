Course Overview

This comprehensive course offers an in-depth understanding of both finance and human resource principles, equipping students with essential skills for informed financial decision-making and effective strategic planning in human resources.


Key Topics:

Financial Analysis: Learn to analyze financial statements, ratios, and performance metrics to make sound investment decisions.


Investment Strategies: Explore various investment options, portfolio management techniques, and risk mitigation strategies.


Human Resource Fundamentals: Understand key HR functions, including recruitment, employee relations, and performance management.


Talent Acquisition and Recruitment: Learn effective strategies for attracting and hiring top talent.


Performance Management: Learn techniques for evaluating and improving employee performance.

What you'll learn

Financial decision-making and investment analysis.

Risk assessment and portfolio management.

Strategic marketing planning and execution.

Digital marketing and consumer insight
