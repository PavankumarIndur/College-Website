Prof. Prashant U.Gujanal

(Lecturer)

About:

Prof. Prashant Gujanal is Lecturer in the Department of Management Studies, SDM College of Engineering and Technology, Dharwad. He has completed his MBA from KLS IMER, Belagavi and has qualified for NET and KSET. Prof. Prashant Gujanal has 3years of corporate experience and 13 years of experience in PG teaching in the dual areas of Marketing and Human Resource. He is a member of Board of Studies and Member Secretary for Board of Examiners of the Department of Management Studies. He has presented 6 conference papers and published 6 papers in Journals. Presently he is pursuing his Ph.D. in Management form RCU, Belagavi.

Qualifications: B.Arts, MBA, NET, KSET
