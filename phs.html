Dr. Prakash H.S.

(Director MBA)

About:

Dr. Prakash. H.S. is currently the Director MBA, Shri Dharmasthala Mnajunatheshwara College of Engineering and Technology. Dharwad. Having Ph.D. in the area of ‘Rural consumer preferences towards consumer durables’ from Mangalore University, Mangaluru. He has earned his Master of Business Administration from Visvesvaraya Technological University, M.Com from Kuvempu University, and qualified UGC-NET under Management. He is having 13 years of Teaching in PG departments and 6 years of corporate experience in the Marketing Management area. Dr. Prakash H.S. has served in the Government of Karnataka as a District level Officer for a short period of time. Dr. Prakash H.S. is a VTU recognized Ph.D. guide and guides one student. his special areas of teaching and research are Marketing Management, Consumer behavior, Operations Management, Rural Marketing, Promotions and Distribution Management. Dr. Prakash H.S. was a guest faculty at the Institute of Charted Accountant of Indian (ICAI), Mangaluru chapter. He has published 13 research papers in various leading and indexed journals and presented research papers in National and International conferences organized by top B-Schools across India like IIM Sirmaur (HP), IIM-K, Nirma University Ahmadabad, Symbiosis Pune, Bharti Vidyapeeth New Delhi and Prestige Institute Indore (MP), etc… He has headed the Industrial Consultancy Cell of SDM PG Centre for the period of 4 years and as a faculty advisor of Rotaract Club of SDM PG Centre Mangaluru successfully organized many events under the banner. Dr. Prakash HS has been invited as a resource person in many National Level Workshops and FDPs across the State. He has been associating with many private and public universities as an external paper setter or external examiner.

Qualifications:MBA, NET, Ph.D., M.Com
