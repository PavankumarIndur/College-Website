Dr. Saleem Sonnekhan

(Assistant Professor)

About:

Dr. Saleem G Sonnekhan is Assistant Professor in Department of Management Studies, SDM College of Engineering & Technology, Dharwad. Affiliated to Visvesvaraya Technological University – Belgavi, Karnataka, India. Dr. Saleem is considered a prominent Educationist, Scholar, and Leader in the field of Strategic Management, Marketing Management, and Mergers and Acquisitions. His work has been published in National and International Journals and three books are in process of publishing.

Qualifications: MBA, Ph.D
