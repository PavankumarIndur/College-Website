Prof. Bharathi V Sunagar

(Lecturer)

About:

Prof. Bharathi V. Sunagar is currently working as a Lecturer in the Department of Management Studies, SDM College of Engineering and Technology, Dharwad. She has completed her Masters of Business Administration from Basaveshwara Institute of Management Studies, Bagalkot in addition she has also completed her Master of Arts in English from Karnataka University, Dharwad. She has qualified for KSET. Mrs Bharathi V Sunagar has 13.6 years of teaching experience. Her areas of interest are Human Resource Management, Talent Acquisition, Organization Development, and Business communication. She is also a trainer in Soft Skills and has experience of training more than 200 students. She is a BOS Member Secretary and Coordinator for Sc-ST Cell of college. She has presented 13 papers at National and International Conferences and also has published 3 research papers in National and International Journals. Presently she is pursuing her Ph.D. in Management from Rani Channamma University, Belagavi.

Qualifications:MA, MBA, KSET(Ph D)
