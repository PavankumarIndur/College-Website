Welcome to the Department of Management Studies.

At Shri Dharmasthala Manjunatheshwara College of Engineering and Technology, Dharwad. Known for its quality management education in Karnataka, the MBA department was founded in 2008 by Rev. President Padma Vibhushan Dr. D. Veerendra Heggade. We excel in teaching, research, and on-site training, aiming to develop competent management professionals with human values. Our lush green campus offers a vibrant environment for interaction and camaraderie. The distinguished faculty, along with visiting experts, guide students with industry-aligned curricula. Our unique blend of theory and practice prepares students for a globally competitive world, resulting in excellent placement records. Our alumni have consistently demonstrated creativity and innovation, achieving outstanding success and recognition.

MBA Master Club

This activity aims to boost students' confidence in public speaking. It helps them quickly organize their thoughts and deliver a well-structured speech. The goal is to prepare students to speak confidently during their final interviews and respond promptly to questions in personal interviews.

Book reading

Incorporating additional activities into book reading sessions is crucial as it deepens engagement and understanding. These activities, such as discussions, creative projects, and interactive sessions, help participants connect more personally with the material, making the reading experience richer and more memorable.

Group discussions

Management Games

This fun event is designed to break the monotony of classroom sessions, keeping students motivated and engaged. Carefully chosen games encourage group participation, helping students get to know each other better, improve communication, and find unconventional solutions to problems.
